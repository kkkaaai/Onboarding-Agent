RASPBERRY COFFEE - COMPANY PROFILE & EMPLOYEE DIRECTORY
=======================================================

COMPANY PROFILE
---------------

Our Identity:
Raspberry Coffee is a design-led technology company that believes technology should 
enrich life without complicating it. We create premium, beautifully crafted devices 
and a seamless ecosystem that work together in harmony. Our name reflects our 
philosophy: the simplicity of a perfect raspberry and the rich, familiar comfort 
of coffee—natural, essential, and a daily joy.

Mission:
To create beautifully intuitive, human-centered technology that empowers creativity, 
fosters connection, and seamlessly integrates into the fabric of daily life.

Vision:
To design and nurture a holistic ecosystem where our devices, software, and services 
work together so flawlessly that they feel like a natural extension of one's self.

What We Do => The Raspberry Ecosystem
-----------------------------------
We don't just sell products; we craft an integrated experience built on three pillars:

Premium Hardware:
- Raspberry Handheld: Flagship smartphone with minimalist design and industry-leading camera
- Raspberry Slate: Powerful laptop/desktop hybrid with haptic feedback keyboard
- Raspberry Watch: Health and wellness companion with clinically-validated metrics
- Raspberry Buds & Beats: Wireless audio with pristine sound quality

Intuitive Software (Bloom OS):
- Clean, minimalist operating system powering all Raspberry devices
- Fluid animations, focus modes, and baked-in privacy

Integrated Services:
- Raspberry Connect: Secure cloud service with end-to-end encryption
- Raspberry Arcade: Curated collection of premium, ad-free games
- Raspberry Stream: Original content focused on creativity and mindfulness

Current Strategic Projects (FY25)
---------------------------------
1. Project Nova: Raspberry Handheld 4 & Bloom OS 4.0
   - Generational leap with new custom "Mocha" chip
   - Contextual intelligence and predictive assistance

2. The Aurora Design System
   - Comprehensive evolution of design language across all products
   - More cohesive and accessible sensory experience

3. Platform Re-Architecture & "Bean" Chipset
   - Fundamental re-architecting for deeper hardware-software integration
   - New family of custom silicon processors

4. Health & Wellness Initiatives
   - New sensor technology for Raspberry Watch
   - Advanced sleep analysis and mental wellbeing features

5. Enterprise & Education Expansion
   - Strategic push into corporate and educational environments

6. SOC2 Readiness & Privacy Infrastructure
   - Enterprise-grade data security for all user information

Our Values: The Core of Raspberry
---------------------------------
1. Simplicity is the Ultimate Sophistication:
   Distilling complex technology into simple, intuitive, and joyful experiences

2. Privacy is a Human Right:
   Products designed to protect user data with transparency

3. Deeply Integrated, Not Merely Connected:
   Magical cohesion between all devices and services

4. Craft in Every Detail:
   Obsession with craftsmanship in materials, code, and user interactions
